Sago Fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked sago pearls (follow package instructions)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder (if using), and salt.
  2. Add cooked sago pearls and mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk and egg (if using).
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. The batter should be thick but pourable. If it's too dry, add a little more milk by tablespoonfuls.
  5. Heat enough oil in a large pan or deep fryer to reach 350°F (175°C).
  6. To form the vadas, you can use two spoons or your hands wet with water. Scoop a spoonful of batter and gently drop it into the hot oil. Alternatively, roll the batter into small balls between wet hands and then flatten slightly before frying.
  7. Fry the vadas for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  9. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce.
